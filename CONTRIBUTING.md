# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in this repository.

This repository does not accept pull requests from outside the organization, so our members can get experience.

If you are seeing this and are a SJSU student, please consider joining our team!

## 🚫 Pull Requests

- Pull requests opened by non-members will be automatically closed.
- Only organization members and approved collaborators may submit PRs.

This policy helps us maintain consistency, security, and internal development workflows.

---

## 📋 Before Opening an Issue

Please:
- Search existing issues to avoid duplicates
- Use clear and descriptive titles
- Keep reports focused and actionable

---

## 🔐 Internal Contributions

If you are part of the organization:

- Create branches from the appropriate base branch
- Follow internal coding and review guidelines
- Ensure all checks and reviews pass before merging

---

## ⚖️ Code of Conduct

By participating in this repository, you agree to follow our Code of Conduct.

---

## ❓ Questions

If you're unsure whether something belongs here, open an issue and ask.

---

Thank you for your understanding.
